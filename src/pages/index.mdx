## AIOps Track

<PageDescription>

This site contains lab tutorials and suplemental materials to be used in the AIOps track during the AIOps Tech Jam 2022.
Most of the tutorials are not exact step-by-step instructions but rather a reference, providing you with the main steps you have to complete to realize the task.
For the rest of information tutorials will point you to the relevant documentation.

The content of the site is organized along the most common task and activities you would have to perform preparing and executing the Proof-of-Value installation of the products from AIOps family - Cloud Pak for Watson AIOPs, Instana and Turbonomic.

</PageDescription>
